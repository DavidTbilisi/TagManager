Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 May 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Short version: TagManager runs entirely on your local machine. Your files and tags never leave your device. We collect only the minimum data required to deliver your license and process payment — nothing more.

1. Who we are

TagManager is developed and maintained by Davit Tchintcharauli, an individual software developer based in Tbilisi, Georgia. Contact: ge.philadelphia1@gmail.com

2. Data we collect

2a. When you use the free CLI

We collect nothing. The CLI operates entirely offline. Your tag database (~/file_tags.json) is stored locally on your machine and is never transmitted to any server.

2b. When you purchase a license

Payments are processed by Paddle.com, our Merchant of Record. Paddle collects the information necessary to process your payment (name, email, billing address, payment method). We receive from Paddle only:

  • Your email address (to deliver your license key)
  • Your order reference number
  • Your license tier and purchase date

We do not receive or store your card number, bank details, or full billing address. Paddle's privacy policy is available at paddle.com/legal/privacy.

2c. When you contact us

If you email us for support, we retain your email address and message content for as long as necessary to resolve your request, and no longer than 2 years.

3. How we use your data

  • To deliver your license key after purchase
  • To respond to support requests
  • To notify you of critical security updates (rare; opt-out available)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the product you purchased.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party, except:

  • Paddle — our payment processor and Merchant of Record
  • Legal authorities — only when required by law and only to the extent required

5. Data storage and security

License and order records are stored in a password-protected environment. We retain purchase records for a minimum of 5 years as required for tax and accounting compliance under Georgian law.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, email ge.philadelphia1@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

This website does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. No analytics scripts are loaded.

8. Children

TagManager is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and announce the change on the GitHub repository.

10. Contact

Privacy questions: ge.philadelphia1@gmail.com
